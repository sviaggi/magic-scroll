FAQ:

Q: How do I download this?
A: On iOS/Android, go to the settings menu of your browser, and scroll until you see something like "Add to home screen." It should work from there!

Q: How do I add songs?
A: Tap the "Songs" button and either click "New" to type/paste chord sheets directly or click-and-drag your .txt or .json files in there to add them!

Q: How do I share songs between devices?
A: Click the ... on the edge of a song folder and select "Export," then send that .json file to the other device! If you want to share your whole library, you can select "Export" at the bottom of the Songs menu and use that file as well. If your song is ready to publish, you can do that as well!

Q: Why are my chord diagrams showing the wrong instrument?
A: By default, they show a guitar in E Standard. You can click the "Diagrams" button to change it to whatever you'd like!

Q: Hey, I think this chord is wrong!
A: My bad! Tell me which and I’ll fix it. Overall I had to hand-program about 155 chords across however many instruments and tunings we have… It was mind-numbing stuff. While I am unconscious of intentional error, I am nevertheless too sensible of my defects.

Q: Can you make it so we can download songs directly from Ultimate Guitar/Musescore/Other services?
A: I’d really like to, but then I would run into this unfortunate issue called That’s Illegal and They’ll Get Me. Nobody can stop you from copy/pasting things in from there and circulating your collections, though! If you’re savvy, there’s always a way. 


Q: Why isn’t [Feature] included?
A: I threw this together over the course of a few commutes and one spirited weekend. I’m not a programmer and my job is in a different field entirely. If a feature is feasible/would improve UX enough I’ll find time to make it happen.
