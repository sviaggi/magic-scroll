☁ Publish to Online Library

Before you publish, please confirm:
  • Publishing is final — the published version can't be changed or removed without an administrator.
  • Don't submit a minor edit or revised version if a similar song already exists in the library.
  • Don't publish something that isn't useful to other players — personal annotations, incomplete drafts, or private variations aren't a good fit. Remember there are offline functions if you need to share something with a small group-- You can just save the file to your device and send it over Whatsapp, Discord, or other messaging apps instead!
  • Make sure the song is complete: lyrics, chords, key/capo info, and any other details should be thoroughly checked before committing.
  • Do not submit copyrighted material. Only publish songs you have the right to share — original compositions, traditional or public domain songs, or content with an open licence.
🔒 Submissions are anonymous — no account, name, or identifying information is attached to anything you publish.
